Our Firm




Freshly imported high quality 'Gada' manufactured with finest cotton in the market is cut in the cutting section as per the requirement, that is later sent to be stiched at an external center to form a final T-shirt product.

T-shirts that arrive as final products are taken to the Ironing section where they are pressed with industry-standard equipments and folded to perfection ready to move to next section.

Neatly folded garments are moved to the packing section where they are enclosed in pristine quality packaging that ensures intactness, preparing them to be exported to our beloved clients.
